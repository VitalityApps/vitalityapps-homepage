Our Company

Based out of Kansas City, we are a software company with a vision to come alongside senior living communities to better serve their residents and families. We are founded by those with experience in the senior living sector, and our goal is to provide a product that makes it easier for staff to communicate with residents, and residents to connect with each other and their families.

We believe that all people can benefit from technology, and we want to put "retirement at your fingertips".
