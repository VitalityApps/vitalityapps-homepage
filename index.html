Smartphone App

As the aging population becomes more comfortable with using handheld devices like smartphones and tablets to keep in touch with friends and family, residents are looking for new ways to engage with your community from their device.
Custom app development is slow and expensive, up to $100K or more, just to launch your app. Using our platform, we can launch your app in just a couple weeks, at a fraction of the cost, and our team will keep it up and running so you don't have to.
  • Custom branding guided by our staff
  • Fully managed web-based administration
  • Available for iOS + Android
  • Coming soon: App analytics, review monitoring, and more!

New Residents

New residents and their families have many options to consider when deciding whether to call your community their home, so make it easy for them by giving the information they're looking for right at their fingertips.
With your contact info in the app, they're just a touch away from speaking with your staff when they're ready to learn more.
  • Virtual tour photos
  • Google Maps integration
  • Quick access to call, e-mail or visit your website

Community Feed

Sending out newsletters is time-consuming and expensive, it doesn't allow you to share that fantastic group photo from the family picnic, and once they've been delivered, it's hard to send reminders to residents for events as they're happening. With quick access to the news and announcements in your custom app, you can give your residents the information they're looking for as it happens.
  • Upcoming events posted by your staff
  • Attach event flyers
  • Send announcements to all residents
  • Share photos from recent events

Activities

Many studies have shown that helping current residents make friends and be an active part of a community boosts overall satisfaction, making them more likely to invite their friends and family to join your community. You put a lot of effort into sponsoring activities for your residents, let us help you spread the word so everyone can join.
  • Customizable print calendars
  • Recurring events (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • View activities in the mobile app

Meals

The dining experience is an important part of our daily life, and your staff works hard provide a balanced diet that catersto a wide array of dietary restrictions. With our meal item tagging features, and menu information available in the mobile app, you can help residents make healthy choices when they eat.
  • Dining menus in the mobile app
  • Menu items grouped by category
  • "Always Available" menu options
  • Highlight special items, ex. gluten free, heart healthy
